# Tier 3 Timing Adjustment for The Folly of Nagakhêdi

We are adjusting the start date for Tier 3 of The Folly of Nagakhêdi. Tier 3 now opens at Noon Eastern (-4 GMT) on Thursday, April 2nd. The Leading the Charge deed will be available to earn until 3:00 AM Eastern (-4 GMT) on May 21st. Read more about Update 47 on LOTRO.com!
