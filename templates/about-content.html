About LOTRO Guides

Welcome to LOTRO Guides — a fan-created resource dedicated to Lord of the Rings Online, the award-winning MMORPG set in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth.

What We Do

We create in-depth guides, walkthroughs, and news coverage to help players of all experience levels get the most out of LOTRO. Whether you're a brand-new hobbit taking your first steps out of the Shire or a seasoned champion tackling endgame raids, you'll find something useful here.

Our content covers:

  • Class Guides — builds, rotations, and gearing advice for every class
  • Crafting Guides — vocations, leveling paths, and tips for turning a profit
  • Instance & Raid Walkthroughs — strategies and mechanics breakdowns
  • Beginner Tips — everything new players need to start their journey
  • Legendary Items — the complete guide to the reforged LI system
  • News & Updates — coverage of patches, events, and expansions

About the Game

Lord of the Rings Online is a free-to-play MMORPG developed by Standing Stone Games. Set in Tolkien's Middle-earth, players explore iconic locations from the books — from the rolling hills of the Shire to the fiery depths of Mordor and beyond. With a rich story-driven experience, deep crafting systems, and a welcoming community, LOTRO continues to be one of the most beloved MMOs since its launch in 2007.

Community Driven

This site is made by the community, for the community. We're passionate LOTRO players who want to share what we've learned and help fellow adventurers across Middle-earth. If you have suggestions, corrections, or want to contribute, we'd love to hear from you.
