# Celebrate Spring in The Lord of the Rings Online! 

The Spring Festival ushers in warmer days and the start of the planting season, and players throughout Middle-earth can celebrate with rewards, quests, and more! The 2026 Spring Festival begins at 10:00 AM Eastern (-4 GMT) on Thursday, March 12th and continues until 3:00 AM Eastern (-4 GMT) on April 1st! This guide will get you started earning Festivity Tokens, Spring Leaves, and other rewards.
