# Paid Transfers Between 64-bit Game Worlds Now Available

Paid transfers are now available between our 64-bit game worlds based on region. Transfers between US worlds, and transfers between EU worlds, is now available. Free transfers from our former 32-bit game worlds will return in the future when these worlds are available as dark worlds. We will have more information on the timing of opening our new dark worlds soon.
