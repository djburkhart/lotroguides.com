## Section One: The Outer Barrow

### Trash Mob Mechanics

Before diving into pull orders, familiarize yourself with the enemy types you will encounter throughout the instance. Each mob has unique mechanics that must be handled correctly to avoid wipes.

> **Important:** Always pull the pathing Deadlight before each group to avoid it summoning more enemies.

#### Decaying Barrow-Oak

- **Sloughing Rot** drops every 15 seconds and subtracts **3% morale per tick**
- **Unearthly Corruption** reflects **20% damage as acid** and needs to be removed immediately
- **Swarming Bees** means leave the group — every 3 seconds it subtracts **10% morale** from the affected player, but if you are standing in the group it subtracts **100% morale**. Only move **3 metres** away from the group
- Below **20% health**, **Huorn's Wrath** activates giving the tree **+25% Melee, Ranged, and Tactical Damage** while increasing its run speed by **15%**

#### Devourers

- **Devourer's Might** should be interrupted immediately when a **yellow bar** appears — otherwise it gains **+50% melee damage** and **+15% run speed** (these buffs can stack)

#### Deadlight

- Pull by itself to prevent it from spawning infinite Grave-weights

#### Grave-weight Warriors

- Remove the corruption **Undying Strength** which grants **+25% melee damage** and **-50% incoming ranged and tactical damage**
- Randomly applies a bleed called **Rend** which **cannot be cleansed** — stack heals on anyone affected as it deals heavy damage

#### Grave-weight Archers

- **Dead Aim** corruption grants **+50% ranged damage** — they should be CC'd or targeted first (this is a **green eye**)
- **Fearful Heart** is a **10-second fear** that can fear you into puddles during the last boss fight — archers must go down first or be CC'd

#### Plaguebearers

- **Touch of Plague** — if you get this **green eye** and don't move out of the group, it subtracts **100% morale** from those around you. Move **5 metres** away
- They have an aura that causes damage and decreases your **Vitality** — only heavy classes should stand near them
- **Breath of Decay** decreases your incoming healing by **-20%** and lasts **20 seconds**

#### Crawlers

- **Crawler's Ire** gives a **red eye** which forces an attack onto the player or pet with the eye
- **Sudden Focus** is a corruption that gives the target **+100% melee damage** and **+40% run speed** — remove it

## Boss 1: Fladach

This is a classic tank-and-spank encounter. The tank holds aggro on Fladach and taunts any crawlers as they spawn. Healers should watch for the random poisoned effect throughout the fight. DPS should only target Fladach.

### Key Mechanics

- **10 seconds** into the fight, crawlers spawn
- Crawlers **explode simultaneously** and drop **Poisoned Earth** which subtracts **20% morale every 3 seconds** (this can stack)
- **Rooted** — interrupt the **yellow bar** induction which causes a wound that roots you in place. If missed, cleanse immediately
- **Rain of Decay** — a **grey bar** distributed induction that applies a poison to everyone. Healers must cleanse as soon as possible
- **Deadly Poison** — a **green eye** applied to **3 random players** that must be potted immediately. It deals massive damage every 3 seconds for 9 seconds
- Fladach will randomly apply a **poisoned effect** throughout the fight that deals massive damage every 3 seconds for 9 seconds to anyone in the group

### Strategy

1. Tank picks up Fladach and holds position
2. DPS burns Fladach — do not switch targets
3. Tank taunts crawlers as they spawn
4. Interrupt **Rooted** (yellow bar) every time
5. Healers cleanse **Rain of Decay** (grey bar) immediately
6. Players with **Deadly Poison** (green eye) use pots immediately
7. Healers keep heavy sustained healing to counter the random poisoned effect

## Boss 2: Luilloth and Astoryn

A dual boss fight. Kill **Luilloth first** and try to AoE Astoryn down alongside her so they both die around the same time.

### Setup

The tank starts the fight where Luilloth spawns, turned away from the door. Everyone else groups up and waits for the **purple eye**. When the purple puddles drop, healers should bubble up and heal through the first eye until the second eye drops.

### Phase Transitions

- **Puddles** — after the purple eye expires, the fellowship groups up and drops puddles together in the **middle of the room**. Each person drops **2 puddles**, so don't move — stack them in one spot
- At **60% boss health**, focus and destroy **Urns** before returning to the boss. Urns must be destroyed within **10 seconds** of spawning or the whole fellowship gets cursed, gaining **+60% melee, ranged, and tactical damage**
- At **33% health**, repeat urns
- When **Astoryn** attacks via the **red eye**, one DPS should focus Astoryn, remove corruptions, and interrupt any yellow bar inductions (especially the **Apprehension** debuff skill)
- For **Blight**, everyone leave the tank and wait for it to expire
- When a **bleed** occurs, pop all health skills

### Key Mechanics

- **Fear-stricken** — interruptible, causes **-40% incoming healing**
- **Blight** — **grey bar** induction that deals damage to anyone near the affected player. Leave the group if you have it
- **Marked Prey** — Astoryn will **force-attack** anyone with the **red eye**
- **Bleeding Wound** — **-15% morale** every 3 seconds for 9 seconds. After expiration, **-20% morale** every 9 seconds. Healers should focus anyone with this wound
- **Retribution** — while urns are active, Luilloth gains **+20% melee, ranged, and tactical damage** and **reflects 100% damage**
- **Loyal Defense** — corruption that tiers up from **1 to 5**, scaling from **-25%** to **-85% incoming melee, ranged, and tactical damage**. Remove immediately before it stacks
- **Astoryn's Induction** — interrupt when the dog randomly targets someone and starts a **yellow bar** to avoid the Apprehension debuff
- **Diseased Wound** — **-8% morale** every 3 seconds for 9 seconds. On expiration, **-12% morale** every 3 seconds for 9 seconds. This comes from Astoryn biting a player — healers be aware
- When **either boss dies**, the surviving one (Astoryn) gains **Undying Rage**: **+60% outgoing damage** and **+30% attack speed**. Mobs will spawn and should be CC'd

## Boss 3: Amondir Hithrengor

A two-phase boss fight with heavy corruption and fear mechanics.

### Phase 1

The tank stays on the platform and moves **corner to corner** dropping puddles. When **Veil of Fear** is active, start removing corruptions immediately while keeping the reflect in mind.

- At **65%** and **36%** health, the boss drops to the floor and you kill **Essences of Fear**
- Interrupt the **yellow bar** which causes a **fear effect**

### Phase 2

**Hope wipe** occurs immediately at the start of Phase 2 — use a **Hope Token** right away.

**Judgment of the Doomsman** is the **purple eye** and drops a massive-damage puddle. Drop puddles as a group. The **2 players without eyes** should focus on interrupts. Healers need to save all group heals for Judgment of the Doomsman. The puddle deals **-20% max morale damage every 3 seconds**.

- At **67%** he says *"Rise, my subjects"* — adds spawn
- At **33%** he says *"The dead obey me"* — more adds spawn

#### Phase 2 Add Wave 1

Plaguebearer → Archers ×2 → Shamblers ×2 → Warriors ×2 → Arms

#### Phase 2 Add Wave 2

Plaguebearers ×2 → Archers ×2 → Shamblers ×2 → Warriors ×2 → Arms

### Additional Mechanics

- **Aggro resets** after Essences die at 10% when he phases, and after the final Judgment Vortex
- **Vortex** — when active, it pulls you toward Hithrengor. Run toward the door until it ends
- **Heart of Darkness** — must be cleansed **3 times** to remove the **-20% max morale** and **-30% incoming healing** debuff
