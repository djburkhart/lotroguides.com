# The Curator is back! Available through September 1st.

The Curator has returned! Find The Curator in Bree, The Twenty-first Hall, Galtrev and other locations throughout the game. Items being added to The Curator’s rewards include Heartwood and Carn Dûm Alchemist cosmetics in addition to an assortment of housing decorations and cosmetic pets!
