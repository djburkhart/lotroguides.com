Spring Festival 2026 — Event Guide & New Rewards

March 27, 2026 Amdor

Spring Festival 2026

The Spring Festival has returned to Middle-earth, running from March 20 through April 12, 2026. This year's festival brings new quests, cosmetics, and quality-of-life improvements to one of LOTRO's most beloved seasonal events.

What's New in 2026

New Quests

"The Hedge Maze of Buckland" — A brand new maze puzzle quest set in the hedge gardens of Brandy Hall. Navigate the winding paths, find hidden clues, and reach the center to claim your prize. Repeatable daily.

"Spring Blossoms of Lothlórien" — Travel to Caras Galadhon and help the Elves prepare their spring bloom ceremony. This quest chain awards a unique Golden Mallorn Sapling housing decoration.

New Cosmetics

This year's festival introduces a beautiful selection of spring-themed cosmetics:

  • Cloak of Spring Blossoms — A flowing green cloak with embroidered flower patterns
  • Circlet of Elanor — A golden headpiece featuring the beloved Lothlórien flower
  • Spring Reveller's Outfit — Full body cosmetic set in pastel greens and golds
  • Festival Pony: Meadow Trotter — A new steed with spring flower decorations

New Housing Items

  • Golden Mallorn Sapling
  • Spring Festival Banner (large & small)
  • Blooming Window Box
  • Festival Lantern Set

Returning Activities

The festival's classic activities all return:

Shrew Stomping (The Shire)

Head to Sandson's Farm and protect the gardens from the shrew invasion! Complete runs quickly for bonus tokens.

Tip: Use the corners of the field to group shrews together. Timing your stomps with the spawn waves maximizes your token earnings.

Horse Races (Bree-land)

The horse racing course in Bree-land returns with its challenging gates and hazards.

Course tips:

  1. Practice the route before timing yourself
  2. Avoid the fence hazards — they slow you drastically
  3. Cut corners where possible
  4. The key is consistency, not speed

Flower Picking (Bree-land Hedge Maze)

Navigate the hedge maze and collect spring flowers before time runs out.

Dance Performances (Various Locations)

Join the dance circles in major festival locations for easy daily tokens.

Festival Tokens & Rewards

Earn Spring Leaves from all festival activities. Exchange them at festival vendors:

Reward Cost
Cloak of Spring Blossoms 50 Spring Leaves
Circlet of Elanor 40 Spring Leaves
Spring Reveller's Outfit 80 Spring Leaves
Festival Pony: Meadow Trotter 120 Spring Leaves
Golden Mallorn Sapling 30 Spring Leaves
Spring Festival Banner (large) 25 Spring Leaves
Assorted Housing Items 10-20 Spring Leaves

Efficient Token Farming

To maximize your Spring Leaves per day:

  1. Shrew Stomping — 3x daily (15 leaves each) = 45 leaves
  2. Horse Race — 1x daily (10 leaves) = 10 leaves
  3. Flower Picking — 3x daily (8 leaves each) = 24 leaves
  4. Dance Quest — 1x daily (5 leaves) = 5 leaves
  5. New Hedge Maze — 1x daily (12 leaves) = 12 leaves
  6. Spring Blossoms chain — Once per character (30 leaves)

Daily total: ~96 leaves (after completing the one-time chain)

At this rate, you can earn enough for the pony mount in about 2 days of dedicated farming.

Festival Locations

  • The Shire — Party Tree, Sandson's Farm
  • Bree-land — Festival Grounds (south of Bree)
  • Duillond — Elf Spring celebrations
  • Celondim — Additional Elf festivities
  • Lothlórien — New 2026 quest location

Don't Miss Out!

The festival ends April 12, 2026. If you want all the new cosmetics and the pony mount, start farming early. The Hedge Maze quest alone is worth experiencing — it's one of the most creative festival additions in recent years.

Happy Spring Festival, adventurers! May your gardens be shrew-free and your token pouch full.
