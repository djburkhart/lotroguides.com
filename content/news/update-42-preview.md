# Update 42 Preview — The Shores of Umbar

Standing Stone Games has revealed details about LOTRO's next major content update, bringing players to the southern coastal regions of Middle-earth. Here's a breakdown of everything announced.

## New Zones

### The Harbor District
The bustling port area of Umbar, filled with merchants, sailors, and intrigue. This zone serves as the main quest hub for the expansion with numerous storylines exploring the Corsair culture and politics.

### The Corsair Isles
A chain of rocky islands off the coast, home to pirate hideouts, ancient ruins, and dangerous wildlife. Features open-world group content and rare spawns.

### The Desert Approach
The arid lands between Harad and Umbar proper. Caravans, oases, and Haradrim camps dot this expansive landscape.

## New Instances

The update includes three new group instances and a 12-person raid:

| Instance | Type | Players |
|----------|------|---------|
| **The Corsair's Redoubt** | Instance | 3-person |
| **Vaults of the Sea-kings** | Instance | 6-person |
| **The Black Serpent's Lair** | Instance | 6-person |
| **Throne of the Storm King** | Raid | 12-person |

The raid features a multi-phase final boss encounter with unique mechanics tied to the naval theme of the expansion.

## Level Cap Increase

The level cap rises from 150 to 155, bringing:
- New skill ranks for all classes
- Updated Legendary Item traceries
- New tier of crafting materials and recipes
- Revised stat curves and gear scaling

## New Features

### Sailing System
A long-requested feature finally arrives! Players can:
- Own and upgrade personal sailing vessels
- Navigate coastal waters between new locations
- Participate in naval skirmishes
- Discover hidden island locations with unique loot

### Reputation Factions

Two new reputation factions:
- **The Free Mariners** — Allies fighting against the Corsairs
- **The Haradrim Wanderers** — A nomadic people open to trade and friendship

Reputation rewards include cosmetics, housing items, and exclusive gear recipes.

## Story Summary

*Without major spoilers:*

The epic quest continues as the forces of the Free Peoples extend their influence southward. With Sauron defeated, the political landscape of Umbar is in turmoil. Former allies of Mordor seek new power, while others look toward peace. Players will navigate complex allegiances, uncover ancient Númenórean secrets, and face a new threat rising from the depths.

## Release Timeline

- **Public Test Server (Bullroarer):** April 2026
- **Expected Live Release:** Late Spring 2026

## Community Reactions

The community response has been overwhelmingly positive, particularly regarding:

1. **Sailing** — The most-requested feature for years
2. **Southern Middle-earth** — Fresh territory away from well-trodden Eriador/Gondor
3. **Group content** — Three instances and a raid at launch addresses content drought concerns
4. **Haradrim inclusion** — Exploring cultures beyond the traditional Free Peoples
