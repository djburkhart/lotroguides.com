# Red Line Hunter End-Game Guide (Post-Rework)

This is an updated **Red Line Hunter** guide for current end-game, reflecting post-rework Hunter skills and raid expectations.

As a Hunter, your job is burst DPS with clean execution and high uptime. This guide focuses on what raid groups expect from Hunters at **level 100+ and end-game**.

## Role and Build Focus

- Primary role: **single-target burst DPS**
- Priority: front-load damage windows and maintain target debuffs
- Raid utility: corruption removal and induction interrupts on demand

## Skills Rotation Tips

### Pre-Encounter Burst Setup

Use this sequence before or on pull:

**Focus -> Camouflage -> Burn Hot -> Penetrating Shot -> Quick Shot -> Swift Bow -> Long Shot -> Barbed Arrow -> Heart Seeker -> Upshot**

### During Encounter Priority

Spam this core sequence:

**Quick Shot -> Swift Bow -> Long Shot -> Barbed Arrow -> Heart Seeker -> Upshot**

Then layer in utility/conditionals:

1. Keep **Penetrating Shot** active as needed to maintain the **-3% Physical and Tactical Mitigation debuff** on target.
2. At targets below 50% morale, add **Bard's Arrow** after **Barbed Arrow**.
3. Use **Orme's Arrow** immediately when corruptions appear.
4. Use **Blindside** to interrupt inductions when required.

## Level 100+ Progression Guidance

The core stat priorities stay the same from 100 onward, but your gearing path changes by bracket.

### Levels 100-119

- Prioritize easy mastery/crit upgrades from quests, instance drops, and crafted pieces.
- Do not overcap one offensive stat while leaving finesse low.
- Start building a clean LI foundation early.

### Levels 120-129

- Tighten crit/devastate balance and avoid finesse drop-off.
- Upgrade traceries consistently instead of waiting for perfect drops.
- Keep mitigation near practical raid minimums, not pure glass-cannon values.

### Levels 130-139

- This is where raid stat discipline starts to matter more.
- Push closer to your target bands and lock in your stable rotation timings.
- Build dedicated single-target and utility variants if needed.

### Levels 140+

- Optimize for current end-game stat caps and encounter demands.
- Keep a survivability variant for progression nights.
- Maintain consumables, corruption response, and interrupt discipline every pull.

## Raid Execution Checklist (Hunter)

Before pull:

1. Confirm stat targets are within acceptable range.
2. Confirm tracery setup for the fight type.
3. Confirm consumables and hope/defensive prep.
4. Pre-plan interrupt and corruption assignments.

During fight:

1. Maintain debuff uptime with Penetrating Shot.
2. Follow burst windows cleanly.
3. Insert Bard's Arrow below 50% morale.
4. Clear corruptions with Orme's Arrow.
5. Interrupt key inductions with Blindside.

## Final Notes

Red Line Hunter remains a high-impact raid DPS role when executed with strong burst sequencing and clean utility timing. If your output feels inconsistent, fix uptime and rotation discipline first, then refine stat tuning.
