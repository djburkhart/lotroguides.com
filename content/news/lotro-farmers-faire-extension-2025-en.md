# Farmer’s Faire available through September 8th!

The Farmer’s Faire is being extended by an additional week, and now ends at 3:00 AM Eastern (-4 GMT) on September 9th. We appreciate your interest in this year’s festival! The festival extension only applies to our 64-bit game worlds, since the 32-bit worlds will no longer be available after September 3rd.
