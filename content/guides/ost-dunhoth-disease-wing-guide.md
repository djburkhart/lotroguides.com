# Ost Dunhoth Disease Wing — Corrupted Huorns

This guide covers the Disease Wing of Ost Dunhoth, from the trash leading up to the Corrupted Huorns boss fight through the T2 Challenge strategy.

## T2 Challenge

**Kill both corrupted Huorns — Baleleaf and Dourbark — within 10 seconds of each other, while only killing two waves of Bog Lurkers (12 total).**

---

## Trash Pulls (Lead-up to Boss)

There are three small pulls of adds before you reach the corrupted trees, consisting of **Kergrim Bone Gnawers**, **Grodbog Workers**, **Grodbog Queens**, and **Deep-worm Scavengers**.

### Mob Breakdown

#### Kergrim Bone Gnawers

- Have the **Anger** buff, rendering them immune to the first crowd control (CC) thrown at them. You must break this buff with one CC skill before a second CC will stick.
- Recommended to use a **daze** to lock these down after breaking Anger.
- After **50 seconds** the Anger buff will reapply — watch timers and be ready to re-CC.
- Have a nasty **distributed hit** — all melee must stack tightly together to split the damage evenly.

#### Grodbog Workers

- Have the **Thickened Shell** buff. As they take damage, their **run speed increases**, making them progressively harder to kite.
- Must be **kept moving** so they cannot induct their larger hits.
- Deal **morale damage to their target equal to 35% of the target's max morale** every 25–30 seconds when in melee range. Extremely dangerous when multiple Workers use this at the same time.

#### Grodbog Queens

- Apply **diseases** to players that must be cleansed before they expire. If a disease expires, it spawns small beetles that deal rapid damage to their target.
- More damaging attacks are **ranged**, so tank them in **melee range**.
- **Reduce their target's Resistance rating** considerably.

#### Deep-worm Scavengers

- Summon **disease fields** that deal damage over time — don't stand in them.
- Each Lord will have two thralls.
- Die relatively quickly and are the lowest-priority mobs in these pulls.

---

### Pull 1

1. **Ranged and healers** stay at the top of the stairs to avoid the Kergrim's distributed damage.
2. **Initiate with CC** to break the Anger buff on the Kergrim.
3. A **Burglar riddles** the farther Kergrim while the tank pulls one away so the group can begin DPS.
4. A **Lore-master applies Sticky Tar** at the base of the stairs to slow the Grodbog Workers.
5. The **kiting tank** taunts the Workers and pulls them back into the entrance cave, kiting them until both Kergrim are dead.
6. **Watch the Anger buff** on the locked-down Bone Gnawer — re-CC before it expires.
7. Once both Kergrim are dead, the kiting tank runs back to the stairwell and the DPS group **single-targets the Workers**. Avoid excessive AoE — if all Workers get speed-buffed they will catch the tank and land unavoidable morale damage.

### Pull 2

1. The kiting tank pulls **one Worker** back to the main hall while the main tank grabs the remaining mobs.
2. **Tank the Queen in melee range** — her more damaging attacks are ranged.
3. **Cleanse all "Impregnated" diseases** immediately or you will spawn multiple beetles with extremely fast attacks.

### Pull 3

1. **Break both Bone Gnawers' Anger buff** and keep one or both locked down while you kill the Queens.
2. **Remove diseases** and step out of disease fields left by the Deep-worms.
3. Tank the Kergrim on the stairs away from the ranged group — make sure melee are stacked for distributed hits.

## Boss Fight: Corrupted Huorns (Baleleaf & Dourbark)

The challenge is a **DPS race** with no hard enrage timer. Split the raid into two balanced groups with roughly even DPS potential.

### Raid Setup

- **Two groups**, one per Huorn (Baleleaf and Dourbark)
- Each group needs: **1 tank, 1 healer, DPS**
- In each group, designate **one DPS as the Bog Lurker coordinator** — this person calls when to stop and start DPS on Lurkers. Pick someone comfortable leading and making quick calls.

### Huorn Mechanics

- Huorns periodically use an **AoE frontal stun** on the tank. Tanks must **face them away** from the group at all times.
- They have strong melee attacks — it is widely considered **easier to ranged-tank them** if possible.
- Huorns will periodically become **unattackable** and spawn Bog Lurkers.

### Bog Lurker Phase

This is the most dangerous and coordination-heavy part of the fight.

1. **Lurkers spawn in groups of 3** around each Huorn.
2. When Lurkers become active, **everyone runs to the middle** and keeps moving until tanks have aggro.
3. **Tanks must swap Lurkers** — pick up the opposing side's Lurkers and bring them to your group.
4. **DO NOT attack Lurkers before the tank swap is complete.** Damaging the wrong-coloured Lurker reflects **25% damage per hit** back to the attacker. This will easily kill AoE DPS.
5. **Absolutely no bleeds on Bog Lurkers** — DoT ticks on wrong-coloured Lurkers during the swap will reflect and kill you.
6. Bring all Lurkers on your side down to roughly **10–20% morale**, then **stop DPS** and wait for the coordinator's call.
7. **All Lurkers must die at the same time.** If they don't, a new wave spawns from the survivors — and you only get two waves for the challenge.
8. Kill Lurkers **near their Huorn** so DPS can quickly switch back to the boss when the burn phase starts.

### Sticky Sap & Colour Mechanic

- Each Huorn applies a **sticky sap disease** that colour-codes you to that side.
- **Never attack the opposing Huorn while you have their sap colour** — this fails the challenge.
- If a group is struggling for DPS and you want to help the other side: intentionally reflect yourself on a Lurker when you have **Desperate Not to Fail (DNF)** or a revive available. This clears your sap and frees you to attack the other Huorn.

### Cooldown Management

There is no hard timer on this fight. When Lurkers are active, you may **keep 2 alive** and whittle them down slowly while waiting on major cooldowns before killing them and restarting the Huorn burn phase.

### Key Reminders

| Rule | Why It Matters |
|------|---------------|
| No AoE/bleeds on Lurkers before swap | 25% reflected damage per hit — instant death |
| Follow your Lurker coordinator | Uncoordinated DPS spawns extra waves, failing the challenge |
| Stack melee on trash Kergrim | Distributed hits will one-shot isolated players |
| Cleanse diseases immediately | Expired diseases spawn beetles or fail mechanics |
| Tanks face Huorns away | Frontal stun hits the entire group otherwise |
| Kill Lurkers near the tree | Faster transition back to boss burn phase |
| Tanks kite Lurkers if healing is strained | Tanking both Lurkers and the Huorn simultaneously can overwhelm healers |

## Recommended Consumables

Ost Dunhoth Disease Wing features heavy disease pressure and distributed damage. Come prepared:

| Consumable | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Luxurious Roasted Oryx Shank (or your class stat food) | Primary stat boost |
| Pot of Mûrai Honey and Oats | Trail food — mitigation buffs |
| Scroll of Mûrai Battle Lore | Offensive mastery |
| Scroll of Mûrai Warding Lore | Defensive mitigation (critical for tanks and Lurker swaps) |
| Mûrai Edhelharn Token | Hope — counters the dread in Ost Dunhoth |
| Greater Mûrai Athelas Essence | Emergency morale recovery |
| Greater Mûrai Celebrant Essence | Power sustain during extended Lurker phases |

> **Tip:** Disease cleanse pots are essential in this wing. The Grodbog Queens and Deep-worm Scavengers apply diseases constantly — have extra pots on your quickbar.
