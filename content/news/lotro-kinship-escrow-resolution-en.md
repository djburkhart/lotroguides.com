# Missing Kinship Escrow Item Returns

We have been working to resolve an issue that caused items in Kinship escrow to not appear following transfer to a 64-bit game world, and we are currently working to restore missing items to players’ original game worlds. Once this work is complete, players will be able to bring these missing items to their destination world by doing a Shared Items transfer from their initial origin world to their current destination world. These items are currently in the process of being restored, so if you see your items returned on your original world, you can engage the Shared Items transfer tool to bring these items to your destination world. We will announce once all of our processing on the various game worlds is complete. Thank you!
